I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Department of Biomedical Informatics, School of Medicine, Emory University, where I am advised by Dr Abeed Sarker.

Prior to this, I earned my PhD from the Centre of Excellence in Artificial Intelligence, IIT Kharagpur, where I was advised by Dr Jiaul Paik. I was honoured with the Women Breakthrough Award 2023 by Falling Walls and Elsevier Foundation for my doctoral research on AI-driven gender bias detection in textbooks.

My current research focuses on AI for Healthcare with an emphasis on NLP for Social Good. You can find my CV here.
